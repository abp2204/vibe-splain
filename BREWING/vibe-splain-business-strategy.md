# VIBE-SPLAIN Business & Market Strategy

## 1. The Core Target Market
Our primary wedge is **mid-market industrial, operations-heavy, and workflow B2B systems**. This includes companies building or modernizing:
* ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)
* CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
* MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems)
* Inventory, scheduling, order management, and field-service systems

We selected this vertical because it offers the perfect mix of **high pain** (mission-critical workflows, database write-heavy) and **lower barriers to entry** compared to heavily regulated healthcare or banking sectors.

## 2. The Core Technical Pain Point
We are not targeting modern companies building new apps. We are targeting companies bleeding money due to **legacy code entanglements**. 

Specifically, our migration path is **Legacy JavaScript to Modern TypeScript**. The industry is full of massive, highly entangled, untyped Node.js/Express and older React monoliths that businesses are desperate to migrate to modern, typed TS, but cannot because manual rewrites are too risky and traditional LLM file-swaps break production.

## 3. The Benchmark Corpus Strategy
To prove VIBE-SPLAIN's value, our public benchmark corpus must reflect this reality. We will not use shiny modern TypeScript repositories (like Cal.com or Twenty). 

**Benchmark Criteria:**
1. Older, messy, highly entangled open-source JavaScript codebases (or repos stuck halfway through a TS migration).
2. They **must** have robust End-to-End (E2E) test suites (like Cypress or Playwright).

We need these E2E tests to act as robot users, driving realistic traffic through our proxy to prove our migrations work safely.

## 4. The Core Sales Pitch (The Wow Factor)
When presenting VIBE-SPLAIN to a CTO or VP of Engineering, our primary metric is the **0% Regression Rate**. 

Our benchmark report must mathematically prove that VIBE-SPLAIN untangled and migrated massive amounts of legacy business logic to TypeScript with exactly zero breaking changes in production behavior. Safety is the ultimate blocker for legacy modernization, and we solve it.
